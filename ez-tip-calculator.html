Quick Tip Guide

  • 10%: Easy to calculate (move decimal point one place left). Minimum tip for basic service.
  • 15%: Standard tip for normal service in many places.
  • 18%: Good service tip (often automatically added for larger groups).
  • 20%: Great service tip, becoming the new standard in many cities.
  • 25%: Excellent service tip or for exceptional circumstances.

When in doubt, 15-20% is usually appropriate for most service situations.
