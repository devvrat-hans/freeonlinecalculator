Chinese Food Delivery Tipping Guide

When tipping for Chinese food delivery, consider these factors:

  • Standard Tipping: 15-20% of the total order is customary
  • Minimum Tips: Even for small orders, a minimum of $3-5 is recommended
  • Distance: Add more if the restaurant is located far from your delivery address
  • Special Requests: Increase tip for special cooking instructions or accommodations
  • Weather: Consider adding extra during bad weather conditions
  • Order Size: Large orders with many items may warrant a higher percentage
  • Delivery Time: Orders during peak dinner hours, holidays, or special events deserve extra consideration

Chinese Restaurant Delivery Considerations

Chinese restaurant delivery has some unique characteristics:

  • Family-Owned Businesses: Many Chinese restaurants are small, family-owned establishments where drivers may be family members
  • Hot Food Delivery: Special efforts are made to ensure food stays hot with insulated bags and careful packaging
  • Special Instructions: Many Chinese restaurants accommodate special requests like "no MSG," spice level adjustments, and allergy concerns
  • Large Order Handling: Chinese food is often ordered for groups, requiring special care in transport
  • Beverage Stability: Careful handling of soup containers and liquid dishes

Your tip acknowledges the extra care taken to ensure your food arrives hot and prepared according to your preferences.

Chinese Food Delivery App vs. Direct Order Tipping

Tipping practices may differ based on how you place your order:

  • Direct Restaurant Order: Tips typically go entirely to the delivery person, 15-20% recommended
  • App-Based Services (DoorDash, UberEats): 15-20% or $5 minimum recommended, as drivers often receive lower base pay
  • Large Platform Orders: Remember that delivery fees usually don't go to the driver
  • Cash vs. App Tipping: Some drivers prefer cash tips as they receive them immediately
  • Cultural Considerations: In some communities, cash tips may be preferred for tax reasons

Regardless of ordering method, remember that delivery personnel rely on tips as a significant portion of their income.
