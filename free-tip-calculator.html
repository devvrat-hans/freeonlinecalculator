Tipping Guide

Not sure how much to tip? Here's a quick reference guide for common services:

Service Type Suggested Tip
Restaurant Server 15-20% of bill
Food Delivery 10-15% of order (minimum $3-5)

Taxi/Rideshare Driver 15-20% of fare
