About Tipping Uber and Rideshare Drivers

Tipping your rideshare driver is a great way to show appreciation for good service. Unlike traditional taxis, rideshare platforms like Uber and Lyft initially didn't include tipping as part of their service, but now they offer in-app tipping options.

While tipping is not mandatory, it's a kind gesture that supports drivers who often rely on tips as part of their income. The standard range for rideshare tipping is typically 10-20% of the fare, depending on service quality and other factors.

Rideshare Tipping Guidelines

Not sure how much to tip your Uber or Lyft driver? Here are some general guidelines:

  • 15-20%: For excellent service - clean vehicle, safe driving, pleasant conversation (if desired), help with luggage
  • 10-15%: For good service - safe driving and a clean vehicle
  • 5-10%: For standard service - got you to your destination safely but nothing exceptional
  • 0-5%: For subpar service - though consider leaving feedback instead if there were issues

Additional considerations that might warrant a higher tip:

  • Driver waited patiently for you
  • Helped with heavy luggage
  • Navigated difficult weather conditions
  • Drove during late hours or peak times
  • Made extra stops not included in the original fare
