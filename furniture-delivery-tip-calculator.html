Furniture Delivery Tipping Guide

When tipping furniture delivery personnel, consider these factors:

  • Flat Rate Tipping: $5-$20 per person for standard deliveries
  • Heavy/Bulky Items: $20-$40 total for large furniture like sofas or beds
  • Assembly Required: Add $10-$20 for items that need assembly
  • Multiple Items: Consider increasing the tip for multiple item deliveries
  • Difficulty: Add more for walk-up apartments, difficult access, or narrow stairways
  • White Glove Service: 15-20% of the delivery fee for premium services
  • Old Item Removal: Add $5-$10 for removing old furniture

Why Tip Furniture Delivery Personnel?

Furniture delivery requires unique skills and involves significant physical demands:

  • Safely lifting and maneuvering heavy, awkward items without damage
  • Navigating challenging spaces like tight doorways and stairs
  • Protecting your floors, walls, and existing furniture
  • Assembly of complex items that may take significant time
  • Removal and proper disposal of packaging materials
  • Working in various weather conditions

A fair tip acknowledges these challenges and the physical effort required.

Tipping Based on Delivery Service Type

Different furniture delivery services may have different tipping expectations:

  • Standard Delivery: $5-$10 per person for threshold delivery
  • Room of Choice: $10-$20 per person for delivery to a specific room
  • White Glove Service: $20+ per person or 15-20% of delivery fee
  • Furniture Store Deliveries: Usually $10-$20 total for 2-person teams
  • Third-Party Moving Services: $20-$40 total or $10-$20 per person

Remember that delivery fees typically go to the company, not the delivery personnel, so tipping directly rewards good service.
