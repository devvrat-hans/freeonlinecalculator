About This Simple Tip Calculator

This calculator is designed to be as straightforward as possible, offering quick calculations with minimal inputs. Simply enter your bill amount, select a tip percentage, optionally split the bill, and get your results instantly.

Common Tip Percentages

While tipping customs vary by location and service type, here are some common percentages used in the United States:

  • 10%: Minimum tip for adequate service
  • 15%: Standard tip for satisfactory service
  • 18%: Good service tip (often the default for groups)
  • 20%: Great service tip
  • 25% or more: Exceptional service tip

How to Use This Calculator

Using this simple tip calculator is easy:

  1. Enter your bill amount in dollars
  2. Click on one of the percentage buttons to select your tip percentage
  3. If splitting the bill, enter the number of people
  4. Click "Calculate Tip" to see your results

The calculator will show your tip amount, total bill, and per-person costs if applicable.
