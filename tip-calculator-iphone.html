About the iPhone-Style Tip Calculator

This calculator offers an iOS-inspired interface for calculating tips quickly and easily. With its clean design and intuitive controls, you can:

  • Use the slider or preset buttons to select tip percentage
  • Split the bill among multiple people
  • Adjust based on service quality
  • Get instant results with a visually appealing layout

Whether you're dining out, ordering takeout, or getting delivery, this calculator provides a familiar iOS experience for determining appropriate tips.

Tips for Using This Calculator

For the best experience with this iPhone-style tip calculator:

  • Slider Control: Drag the slider to adjust tip percentage in 1% increments
  • Quick Presets: Tap the percentage buttons for commonly used tip rates
  • People vs. Service: Switch between splitting by people count or adjusting by service quality
  • Custom Options: Enter exact values for complete control over your calculations

The calculator is designed to be lightweight and responsive, mimicking the look and feel of native iOS applications.
